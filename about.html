About Us

Our Story

Sanag Restaurant opened in 2015 with one goal: to bring the true flavors of Somali cooking to Minnesota. Our founder grew up in the Sanag region of northern Somalia, learning recipes that had been cooked in the family for three generations.

What started as a small family kitchen has grown into a place the community calls home. Every dish we serve is made the same way it always has been, with the same spices, the same slow cooking, and the same generous spirit that Somali hospitality is known for.
